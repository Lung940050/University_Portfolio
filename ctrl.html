What is CTRL+ ?

CTRL+ is a course designed with a focus on understanding about e-waste.

In 2019, 53.6 million tons of e-waste was generated globally, and the reuse rate was only 17.4%. However, most environmental education in Taiwan does not mention electronic products. Many people do not know the whereabouts and correct recycling channels of electronic products that are recycled and discarded. Therefore, I designed an e-waste lesson plan to let students understand the impact of e-waste on the environment and society and the correct recycling channels, And then deeply rooted in the importance of the concept of environmental protection.

CTRL+ Z/X/C/V

Course 1 - What is e-waste

Understand what is e-waste and e-pollution, and what kind of impact and harm e-waste has on human society and nature, and resonate with students by focusing on life issues.

Course 2 - What can we do for the earth

Understand the main origin and reasons of e-waste, know why to recycle and reduce e-waste, and understand the recycling channels of various electronic products.

Course 3 - We and E-waste

Use the role-playing method of board games to let students think about the relationship between their own consumption behavior and the environment in the experience.

In the course 3, we use discarded circuit boards as the game map to think about the relationship between us and the environment in the form of board game role-playing. During the game, players will act as environmentalists, black-hearted merchants and civilians respectively, collect waste through strategies, and direct the game to their own goals one by one.

Number of players - 6~10
The best number of players - 6~8
Suitable age - 12 years old and above
Play time - 30~40 minutes
Game type - strategy, environmental awareness

Board game rules

Pre-game

Spread out the game map.

Part placement

Place the parts on the blue dots of the game platform, about 20 pieces can be placed.

Draw a role card

One person plays one role.

Draw function card

One person draws five function cards.

Start the game

The unscrupulous factory manager starts the game for the first step, and then proceeds to the right in sequence.

Dice

Take turns to determine the number of steps taken by the dice, and the player will take away the parts encountered on the road.

Use cards

At the end of your turn, you can choose whether to use feature cards.

End of each round

At the end of the round, everyone except the factory director must select a card from their hand and give it to the factory director.

game over

The game is over and the score is settled until all the parts are used up.

Map introduction

The empty circuit map area, where the obtained parts can be placed.

After the game is over, you can store the parts and cards here.

When the game is starting, the player-controlled piece passes through a dot with a part, the player obtains the part.

Card introduction

Character card

Each character has its own character description and exclusive skills.

Item card

The event card is adapted from what mayhappen in the real world, making the overall game closer to reality.

Event card
